My research focuses on the acquisition of relational rules, using computational models to study learning across adults, children, and non-human primates.

**Relational Reasoning** — How do humans (and other primates) learn and generalize abstract relations?

**Discovery Learning** — What computational principles underlie self-directed learning from experience?

**Cognitive Development** — How do learning mechanisms change across development?
